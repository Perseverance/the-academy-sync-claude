5. Voice & Tone

Our brand voice is direct, confident, and authoritative. It's like a tough but fair coach: knowledgeable, encouraging, and always pushing you to be better.

Principles

  • Direct & Confident: Use strong, clear language. Get to the point.
  • Authoritative: Write as an expert. Be declarative and informative.
  • Tough but Fair: Acknowledge challenges. Motivate, don't coddle.
  • Concise: Avoid jargon and filler words. Every word has a purpose.

Examples

  • "You might want to maybe consider trying the next lesson if you have time."
  • "Mastered this? Advance to the next challenge."
  • "It looks like you might have gotten this answer wrong. That's okay!"
  • "Incorrect. Analyze your mistake and try again."
